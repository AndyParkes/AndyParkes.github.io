About South Tyneside Green Party Skills Auction

This skills auction is part of a drive to raise funds for our campaign to get Rhiannon Curtis elected in the ward of 'Beacon and Bents'. We think that this will be a fun way to raise money for our local Green Party as well, creating the potential for some of our Green Party members and supporters to meet each other.

The auction will be open to bids and new skill offers until ... When the auction closes, winning bidders will be put in touch with the person(s) offering the craft/skill and sent a donation form so they can directly donate the value of their bid to South Tyneside Green Party. The winning bidders will also be announced at someGig.

Who can participate in the auction?

All local Green Party members and registered supporters will be informed about the skills auction, but please feel free to invite your friends and family to join in also.

How to bid

Send an email to Andy (see the PDF poster Colin sent you for the email address), quoting 'SKILLS AUCTION: BID' in the subject line. In the email please give:
  1. Your name (only your initials will be shown on this web-page)
  2. The ID number of the craft/skill you are bidding on
  3. The value of your bid

How to offer a craft/skill for auction

Again, send an email to Andy (see the PDF poster Colin sent you for the email address), quoting 'SKILLS AUCTION: OFFER' in the subject line. In the email please give:
  1. Your name
  2. A description of the craft or skill you wish to offer (if its a craft item, you may also like to attach a photograph)
  3. The value of the starting bid

Retracting a bid

If for any reason you wish to cancel your bid, email Andy and the previous bid (if any) will be restored as the high bid.
